About Me

Hello, I’m Jessie Daryl Cacafranca, a Designer and Front-End Developer specializing in Web Design and Digital Media.

In 2006, I earned my diploma Major in Multimedia and the Internet at Informatics SM City Cebu which I had an award of being the Best in Multimedia. Since then, I started doing some freelance work with some local clients here in Cebu. After college, I was hired in a web development company; which i hone my skills in terms of concepts, color theory, typography, designs, coding standards, and flexibility.

As for my first job, I did a lot of corporate designs and learned XHTML/CSS. Having experienced from my previous companies, later on I moved on to an entertainment industry with over two years experienced in a related field. The next step of my career was moving to a Danish web development agency as a Web/Graphic Designer and later on promoted to an Acting Project Manager. Furthermore, my career bloomed and relocated to the Middle East working as a Web Designer/Photographer of a major holding company in Saudi Arabia.

I always into learning new things when it comes about web. It will be my playground to enhance every aspects as a creative designer.

My portfolio has been nominated and featured by several CSS awards websites including CSSAWDS.com, Boostlizer.com, Awwwards.com, Bootstrap Expo, BootCSS Expo, CSSWinner.com, CCSDA, BWDA, CSSLight.com, BestCss.in, BestHTML5Gallery.com, CSSReel.com, NiceOneILike.com, CSSCollector.com, CSSGarden.co.uk, CSSNectar.com, CSS-Fox.com
